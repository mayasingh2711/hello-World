Now in order to have more energy in your whole day then you should save your energy in order to become more confident more enthusiastic in the morning. You have to to save your energy by not using your mobile phone.

According to Dr Nikole Benders-Hadi , a psychiatrist, “immediately turning to your phone when you wake up can start your day off in a way that is more likely to increase stress and leave you feeling overwhelmed.”

Interestingly our mobile phones can directly kill our productivity and creativity just with one wrong message in the early morning. So you have to promise yourself from this very day that you are not going to touch your white tiny screen in the very beginning of a day.
